Warranty Policy

Comprehensive Watch Warranty

We stand behind the quality of every timepiece we sell

Warranty Coverage

All watches purchased from our store come with a comprehensive warranty that covers manufacturing defects and mechanical failures under normal use conditions.

Warranty Periods

  • Luxury Watches: 2-5 years
  • Sport Watches: 2-3 years
  • Classic Watches: 2 years
  • Digital Watches: 1-2 years

What's Covered

  • Manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship
  • Mechanical movement failures
  • Crown and pushers functionality
  • Water resistance (as specified)
  • Crystal defects
  • Bracelet/strap manufacturing defects

What's Not Covered

  • Damage from accidents, misuse, or normal wear
  • Scratches on case, bracelet, or crystal
  • Damage from water exposure beyond specified limits
  • Battery replacement (for quartz watches)
  • Damage from unauthorized repairs
  • Cosmetic aging of materials

Warranty Service Process

1. Contact Us

Call or email our support team with your warranty claim

2. Send Watch

Ship your watch to our service center with provided instructions

3. Repair

Our certified technicians will repair or replace your watch

International Warranty

Our warranty is valid worldwide. You can get service at any authorized service center globally. Keep your purchase receipt and warranty card for service.
