About Rolex

For over a century, Rolex has been synonymous with precision, luxury, and innovation. We craft timepieces that stand the test of time, combining traditional craftsmanship with cutting-edge technology.

Our Story

Founded in 1905, Rolex began as a vision to create the perfect wristwatch. From our humble beginnings in London to becoming the world's most recognized luxury watch brand, our journey has been marked by groundbreaking innovations.

Today, we continue to push the boundaries of horological excellence, creating timepieces that are not just instruments of time, but symbols of achievement and success.
